Pokemon Go

Pokemon Go is a game created based on the show "Pokemon". Its objectve is to catch different pokemon through the use of mobile devices and moving from one place to another. It simulates how people catch pokemon in the anime. Players can also do other stuff like battle other pokemon and join different leauges.
